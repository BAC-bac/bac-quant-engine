# BAC Quant Engine — Research Principles

## 1. Philosophy

This project is built on the belief that:

> Markets are complex, adaptive systems that cannot be reliably predicted, but can be studied, stress-tested, and navigated through robust design.

The goal is not to forecast outcomes with certainty, but to build systems that perform under uncertainty.

---

## 2. Core Principles

### Robustness over Optimisation

Highly optimised strategies often fail in live conditions.

This project prioritises:

- stability across time
- performance across multiple markets
- resistance to overfitting

---

### Risk Management over Prediction

The focus is not on being right, but on surviving being wrong.

Key ideas:

- drawdown control
- position sizing
- capital preservation
- asymmetric risk profiles

---

### Convexity over Consistency

Consistent small gains can hide catastrophic risk.

The project explores:

- small losses with occasional large gains
- tail-risk strategies
- payoff asymmetry
- optionality

---

### Regime Awareness over Static Models

Markets behave differently under different conditions.

This project studies:

- volatility regimes
- trending vs ranging environments
- structural breaks
- macro-driven shifts

---

### Data-Driven over Narrative-Driven

All conclusions must be supported by data.

- backtesting
- statistical analysis
- reproducibility
- measurable outcomes

---

## 3. Research Approach

The workflow follows a structured pipeline:

1. Data collection
2. Data cleaning and standardisation
3. Exploratory data analysis
4. Feature and signal development
5. Backtesting
6. Risk evaluation
7. Reporting and documentation

Each stage must be reproducible and transparent.

---

## 4. What This Project Avoids

- curve fitting to historical data
- single-strategy dependency
- reliance on one market or timeframe
- over-complicated models without clear benefit
- prediction without risk control

---

## 5. Intended Outcome

The end goal is to build:

- a robust research engine
- a portfolio of tested strategies
- a framework for continuous improvement
- a professional body of work demonstrating real quantitative skill
