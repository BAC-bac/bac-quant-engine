# BAC Quant Engine

**BAC Quant Engine** is a personal quantitative research platform for exploring financial markets, sports betting markets, regime behaviour, risk management, and systematic strategy development.

The project brings together data engineering, exploratory analysis, backtesting, portfolio research, and practical risk controls into one structured research environment.

## Project Purpose

The purpose of this project is to build a professional research engine capable of:

- collecting and cleaning financial and sports market data
- analysing market behaviour across different regimes
- testing trading and betting strategies
- studying convexity, drawdown, volatility, and risk-adjusted returns
- developing robust research workflows
- producing clear reports, dashboards, and written analysis

## Core Research Areas

### Financial Markets

This includes research into:

- Forex
